Led by Doc Rock

Doc Rock “shares with ease an amazing store of knowledge about the Earth's long and impressive geological story, explaining what we have seen before but now will see again for the first time. She is excellent in weaving together diverse and fascinating fields and themes beyond geology – such as natural sciences, psychology, human development, group dynamics, creative arts, mythology, physical health, mental well-being, and spirituality – to produce a deeper understanding and appreciation of our intimate human relationship to the Earth. Her natural interpersonal style enhances the effectiveness of the nature program’s total experience, which she infuses with sincerity, sensitivity, caring, graciousness, curiosity, joy, humor and charm.”

~ Larry J. Rosenberg, Ph.D., Chief Empowerment Officer, Globond International, Sedona, AZ
