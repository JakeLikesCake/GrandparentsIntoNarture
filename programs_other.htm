Three-Hour Workshops, Keynote Speeches and Conferences

Three-Hour Workshop

The Three-Hour Workshop includes an introduction to the guiding principles of Sharing Nature with your Grandkids, fun-filled engaging ways to experience Nature more deeply, as well as a discussion on the relevance of Nature to everyday life.


Grandparents Into Nature Retreat Center

Located 20 minutes from Washington, D.C. in naturally beautiful Great Falls, Virginia is training and retreat center created by Doc Rock as a place to experience many ways of deepening your enjoyment of Nature and your bond with your grandchildren, as well as meet new and interesting fellow grandparents from all over the world.


Keynote Speaker or Conference Presenter

Doc Rock (Dr. Barbara Joyce Frank) is available as a Keynote Speaker or Conference Presenter on how active grandparents can become closer to their precious grandkids by exploring Planet Earth together. Her guidance enables grandparents to access Nature's wisdom. Then grandparents can guide their grandkids through a mutual adventure. The happy outcome is a bonding across the generations -- learning, enjoying and loving for all. She is available as a Keynote Speaker or Conference Presenter.
