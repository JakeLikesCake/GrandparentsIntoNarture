One-Day Workshop

Grandparent Nature Wisdom Program

The One-Day Workshop is filled with fun and engaging interactive nature-based low-impact activities.

Morning Session (9 am-12 noon)

Grandparents Knowing Nature

  • guiding principles of Earth Wisdom

  • new and profound ways to experience Nature

  • relevance of Nature to everyday life

Lunch: 1 hour

Afternoon Session (1-4)

Grandparents Sharing Nature with a Grandchild

  • guiding principles in making science an experience

  • telling earth stories

  • developing earth stewardship
